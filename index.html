Must Try

The Goan Classics

Kingfish Thali

The ultimate Goan experience — kingfish curry, rice, sol kadhi and all the accompaniments. Ask us for today's price.

Anda Thali

A comforting Goan egg thali featuring boiled or curried eggs, rice, dal, and vegetable sides — a simple, satisfying meal with authentic Goan flavors.

Bangda Thali

Fresh mackerel (bangda) cooked Goan-style, served as a complete thali with fish curry, rice, sol kadhi, and seasonal vegetable accompaniments — a staple of coastal Goan cuisine.

Chicken Thali

A hearty Goan-style chicken thali featuring spicy chicken curry, steamed rice, dal, and traditional accompaniments — a satisfying, flavor-packed meal rooted in authentic Goan home cooking.

Veg Thali

A wholesome vegetarian Goan thali with seasonal vegetable curries, dal, rice, papad, and pickle — perfect for those seeking authentic vegetarian food in Goa.

Prawns Rava Fry

Juicy prawns marinated in traditional Goan masala, rolled in rava, and fried until golden and crispy — a favorite seafood starter at Goan restaurants.

Bangda Fry

Whole mackerel (bangda) marinated in the iconic Goan recheado masala — a fiery red spice paste — and pan-fried until crispy outside and tender inside. A true Goan classic.

Squid Masala Fry

Fresh squid rings tossed in a spicy Goan masala and pan-fried to perfection — a flavorful seafood dish that's a highlight of authentic Goan cuisine.

Chicken Xacuti

A rich, aromatic Goan chicken curry made with roasted coconut and a traditional spice blend (xacuti masala) — slow-cooked for deep, warming flavor, best enjoyed with rice or bread.
